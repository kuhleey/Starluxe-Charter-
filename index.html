Our Story

After decades of growth as Eldo Coaches, our exciting rebrand marks a new chapter. This is how Starluxe Executive emerged as an independent, stand-alone brand.

The COVID-19 pandemic sparked a radical shift in our decades-old model. Embracing uncertainty, we departed from tradition while upholding our timeless principles.

Anticipating future needs, we forged ahead, embracing discomfort to innovate and create the unprecedented. This transformative journey harnessed partnerships and solidified us as pioneers in the executive coach and charter passenger transport industry.
