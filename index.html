About Starluxe exclusive

Starluxe exclusive emerged from the transformation of Eldo Coaches during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic. What began as a necessity became an opportunity to redefine luxury transportation in South Africa.

Today, we stand as a testament to resilience and innovation in the coach travel industry, combining decades of experience with a fresh approach to customer service.

As a 100% Black-owned enterprise operating under the esteemed David Bus Services (established in 1956), we bring together tradition and modernity to create exceptional travel experiences.

Our Mission & Values

Excellence in Service

We go beyond transportation to create memorable experiences for every passenger, with attention to detail that sets us apart.

Commitment to Safety

Our rigorous maintenance programs and driver training ensure that safety is never compromised.

Community Impact

As a Level 1 BBBEE certified company, we're proud to contribute to South Africa's economic transformation.

Our Services

Starluxe exclusive offers comprehensive transportation solutions tailored to meet diverse needs across South Africa.

Airport & Hotel Transfers

Seamless transportation to and from major airports and hotels, with meet-and-greet services available upon request. Our drivers monitor flight schedules to accommodate delays.

  • OR Tambo International Airport
  • Cape Town International Airport
  • King Shaka International Airport
  • All major hotel chains and conference centers

Corporate Event Transportation

Reliable group transportation for corporate events, conferences, and team building activities. We offer customized schedules and branded vehicles for your company.

  • Executive shuttle services
  • Multi-day event transportation
  • Dedicated vehicles with WiFi
  • Customized routing

Wedding & Special Occasion Charters

Make your special day unforgettable with our luxury wedding transportation. We coordinate with event planners to ensure perfect timing and elegant arrivals.

  • Bridal party transportation
  • Guest shuttles
  • Decorated vehicles

Tour Group Transportation

Explore South Africa in comfort with our tour group services. We work with tour operators to provide knowledgeable drivers and comfortable coaches for extended journeys.
